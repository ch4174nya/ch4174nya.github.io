electronic- Bill of Lading

I work at the IBM Center for Blockchain Innovation (ICBI) along with several other researchers, ideating and solving problems faced by enterprises, particularly in the supply chain space.
Recently, I was engaged in designing and implementing an electronic-Bill of Lading (BL) with Pacific International Lines (PIL). The key aspect about the BL in the ocean frieght ecosystem is that it is a document of title. The electronification of the BL involved abstracting properties of the physical paper BL onto a JSON that'd rest on ledger. This electronic BL would additionally be bound by the dynamic privacy and confidentiality constraints, as dictated by transfer and surrender operations.

Projects

SiegeBreaker

SiegeBreaker is a censorship circumvention system, that leverages Software Defined Networks for a modular architecture. While Tor is based on onion routing, SiegeBreaker implements decoy routing (aka, refraction networking). The key purpose utilizing the SDN architecture is modularity through separation of concerns.
I've been engaged with SDN aspect of the signaling protocol that SiegeBreaker employs.


Preliminary papers on this thread: Towards Practical Infrastructure for Decoy Routing (Positional Paper),
Towards Secure Decoy Routing by Using SDN
